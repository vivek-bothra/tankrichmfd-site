Step 2: Account Activation

Different Paths Based on Your Status

How it works:

  • Our partner uses CAMS to verify your e-KYC status
  • Process varies for Resident vs Non-Resident customers
  • Most cases are automated for faster processing

For Resident Customers

Automatic Processing (Most Cases)

  • Auto-approved for most resident customers
  • Instant activation when e-KYC is complete

Manual Verification (When Required)

  • Re-KYC using Digilocker
  • Quick Help Guide Available
Timeline: Days 1-2

For Non-Resident Customers

Three Pathways Based on Your KYC Status

⚡ Pathway 1: Existing MF Investors

For customers who already have active MF investments

  • KYC already verified online
  • Simple bank statement upload
Timeline: 2 business days

📱 Pathway 2: First-Time with e-Aadhaar

New investors who can receive OTPs on Aadhaar-linked mobile

  • Manual document upload (online)
  • e-Aadhaar verification through OTP
Timeline: Up to 7 days

📮 Pathway 3: Traditional KYC

When e-Aadhaar access is not available

  • Offline KYC process
  • Documents sent to Chennai via courier
Timeline: 30-45 days

  • Foreign Address Proof (photocopy)
  • Signature Sample - Optional on need basis
  • OCI Card (photocopy, if applicable)
  • KYC Form (to be couriered to Chennai)
