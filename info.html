Getting Started Is Simple

Our 3-step process for MF, FD and NPS:

  1. Account creation
  2. Account activation
  3. Investment ready

Step 1: Account Creation

  • Full name (as per PAN card)
  • PAN details
  • Indian mobile number (can be a friend’s number)
  • Email address for notifications

Timeline: Day 1

Step 2: Account Activation

Residents

  • Mostly automatic via e-KYC (Days 1–2)
  • Manual DigiLocker re-KYC only if required

Non-Residents

  1. Existing MF investors – 2 business days
  2. First-time with e-Aadhaar – up to 7 days
  3. Traditional offline KYC – 30-45 days

Required Documents

Resident Clients

  • Aadhaar & PAN
  • Passport-size photo (selfie OK if DigiLocker used)
  • Cancelled cheque & signature sample (only if needed)

Non-Resident Clients

Pathways 1 & 2 (online):

  • PAN & e-Aadhaar
  • Passport, foreign address proof, bank statement
  • OCI card (if applicable)

Pathway 3 (offline): photocopies + couriered KYC form.

Timeline Overview

  • Account creation: Day 1
  • Information update: Day 1
  • Activation: Residents Day 1–2; NR varies (2 days → 45 days)
  • Investment ready: Residents Day 1–4; NR varies accordingly
