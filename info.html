Getting Started Is Simple

Our three‑step process makes opening an investment account straightforward for residents and non‑residents.

Step 1: Account Creation

Provide your full name (as per PAN), PAN details, an Indian mobile number (this can be a friend’s number if you don’t have one), and an email address for notifications. We’ll input the details on a call, and you’ll receive a link via SMS or email for approval.

Timeline: Day 1

Step 2: Account Activation

Residents: e‑KYC via DigiLocker usually completes automatically in 1–2 days. Manual re‑KYC is required only in some cases.

Non‑Residents: Existing mutual fund investors: 2 business days. First‑time investors using e‑Aadhaar: up to 7 days. Offline KYC: 30–45 days.

Step 3: Investment Ready

Once your account is activated and information updated, you’re ready to invest. Explore mutual funds, fixed deposits and NPS options with guidance from our team.

Required Documents

Resident Clients

  • Aadhaar and PAN
  • Passport‑size photo (selfie if using DigiLocker)
  • Cancelled cheque (for bank verification)
  • Signature on a plain sheet (if needed)

Non‑Resident Clients

  • PAN and e‑Aadhaar
  • Passport and foreign address proof, bank statement
  • OCI card if applicable
  • Signature and copies of documents (for offline KYC)
